Welcome

MATHEMATICAL ONCOLOGY RESEARCH

Mathematical and computational modeling approaches have been applied to every aspect of tumor growth from mutation acquisition and tumorigenesis to metastasis and treatment response. My research focuses on developing mathematical approaches that are able to address critical questions associated with vascular tumor progression and targeted therapeutics.
